=abstract start

On <a href="http://search.cpan.org/">search.cpan.org</a> every CPAN author, every person with a <a href="http://pause.perl.org/">PAUSE</a>
account has a page. This page has a URL in the form of <hl>~username</hl>. In this screencast we take a look at the
content of this page.
