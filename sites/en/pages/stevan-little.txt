<ul>
 <li>Stevan Little on <a href="https://github.com/stevan">Github</a>. He works at <a href="http://www.iinteractive.com/">Infinity Interactive</a>.</li>

 <li><a href="https://github.com/MoeOrganization/moe">Moe</a>, an -OFun prototype of an Ultra Modern Perl 5. (Written in Scala.) </li>

 <li><a href="http://www.perl6.org/">Perl 6</a></li>
